Which of the following statements are correct about Plasma Arc Machining (PAM)?

Correct answer: N/A
Solution

(a, b)
In plasma arc machining, the gases are ionized by placing an arc across the path of the gas flow. The gas molecules get dissociated causing large amounts of thermal energy to be liberated. This generates temperature of the order of 16500°C. Which are then utilized in removing metal by melting and vapourization. It is used only for electrically conductive metals. This process is fast, the kerf width is small and the surface finish is good.
