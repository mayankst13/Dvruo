In context of six sigma project methodology, DMADV is used in place of DMAIC:

Correct answer: D
Solution

(d)
Sometimes, the demands of the customers with respect to quality cannot be satisfied by the existing
process. At times, an organization must decide to launch a new product or service to grab a new
business opportunity offered by the environment. In all such situations, the last two steps used in
the DMAIC, namely “improve” and “control” can be replaced by “design” and “verify” so that it
becomes DMADV.
