Which one of the following forecasting method relies on a panel of experts being interrogated by a sequence of questionnaires in which response to the questionnaire is used to produce the next questionnaire?

Correct answer: D
Solution

(d)
Delphi method : One of the most commonly used qualitative forecasting technique is the Delphi method. This technique attempts to develop forecasts through “group consensus.” In this method the experts are not known to each other and are asked to respond to a series of questionnaries. The responses from the first questionnaires are tabulated and used to prepare a second questionnaire that contains the information and opinion of the entire group. Each respondent is then asked to reconsider and possibly review his or her previous response in light of the group information provided. This process continues until the coordinator feels that some degree of consensus has been reached.

Which one of the following is a key principle of lean manufacturing?

Correct answer: C
Solution

(c)
Lean manufacturing is a production methodology focused on minimizing waste and maximizing efficiency. Single-piece flow is a core principle of lean manufacturing where items moves through the production process one at a time rather than in batches. This approach reduces waiting times, inventory levels, and overall lead times, leading to improved productivity and responsiveness to customer demands. Hence, single-piece flow is a fundamental aspect of lean manufacturing practices.
