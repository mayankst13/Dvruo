Q9 Not checked

Not attempted

Which of the following statement(s) is/are correct regarding disk brakes?

Correct answer: N/A
Solution

(b, c, d )
The disk brakes have following advantages:
1. Disk brake is simple to install and service.
2. Disk. brake has high torque transmitting capacity in small volume.
3. In drum brakes, as the temperature increases, the coefficient of friction decreases. Due to inherent ability of disk brake to dissipate heat, it is insensitive to changes in coefficient of friction. There is no, mechanical ‘fade’ of friction lining. Fading is the loss of torque
transmitting capacity of friction lining at higher temperatures.
4. The disk brake is easy to control.
5. The brake can never become self-locking.
6. The brake is equally effective for both directions of rotation of the disk.
7. The disk brake has ‘linearity’, that is, the braking torque is linearly proportional to the actuating force.
