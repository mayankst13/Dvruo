The self sharpening tendency of a conventional grinding wheel does not depend on

Correct answer: N/A
Solution

(a, c, d)
Self-sharpening of grinding wheel takes place when the worn-out abrassive grains break off due to high grinding force and the new grains come out. This property is dependent on the type of bond (soft or hard) and hence wheel grade.
