Not attempted

The Heisler charts are extensively used to determine the temperature distribution and heat flow rate when

Correct answer: D
Solution

(d)
• Heisler charts are used when biot number is greater than 0.1 and lumped analysis cannot be applied.
• When biot number is greater than 0.1, the internal temperature gradient are not small and so internal resistance cannot be neglected.
So, Heisler charts are used when both conduction and convection resistance are almost of equal importance.
