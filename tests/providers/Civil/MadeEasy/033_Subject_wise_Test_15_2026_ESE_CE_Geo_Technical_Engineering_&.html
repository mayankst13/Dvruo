Q9 Not checked
Technical • CIVIL ENGINEERING • +2 / -0.66
Not attempted

Consider the following statements:
1. Standard penetration test (SPT) is conducted by using split spoon sampler.
2. Dynamic cone penetration test for site investigation is based on the principle that elastic shock waves travel in different materials at different velocities.
3. Use of N-values not corrected for overburden pressure leads to highly conservative design of footing at shallow depths.
Which of the above statements are CORRECT?

Correct answer: C
Solution

(c)
Standard Penetration Test (SPT) gives N-value which can be corrected for energy, overburden pressure and dilatancy in sequence. The corrected N-value is correlated with relative density and angle of shearing resistance for cohesionless soils; and unconfined compressive strength and consistency for cohesive soils.
Since the confining pressure increases with depth, the N values at shallow depths are underestimated
and the N values at larger depths are overestimated.
