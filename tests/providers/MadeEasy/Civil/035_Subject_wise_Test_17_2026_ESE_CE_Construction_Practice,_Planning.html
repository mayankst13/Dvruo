Q5 Not checked
Technical • CIVIL ENGINEERING • +2 / -0.66
Not attempted

The problem of developing low-cost housing for economically backward people in an undeveloped region should be analyzed using:

Correct answer: A
Solution

(a)
A GANTT chart is a simple bar chart that visually represents tasks against time. It is particularly
suitable for straightforward projects like low-cost housing in undeveloped regions because it is
easy to understand, implement, and communicate to stakeholders with varying levels of expertise.
Unlike network techniques (CPM or PERT), which are better for complex projects with intricate
interdependencies, a GANTT chart provides a clear timeline for task completion without the need
for detailed dependency analysis. This simplicity makes it ideal for projects where resources are
limited and the tasks are relatively simple and well-defined.

In construction estimation, how is the length of a short wall calculated when using the center-line method?

Correct answer: B
Solution

(b)
In the center-line method, the length of a short wall is calculated as the centre-to-centre distance
between walls minus the breadth (width) of the adjacent wall. This adjustment prevents doublecounting
where two walls share a common center-line.

Correct answer: C
Solution

(c)
• A core assumption of PERT’s probabilistic time estimating is that the probability of achieving

• This assumption is a fundamental part of what makes PERT probabilistic (stochastic), not
deterministic. Deterministic models (like CPM) use a single, fixed time estimate. PERT’s use of
three time estimates is specifically for dealing with uncertainty.
