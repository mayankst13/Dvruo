Not attempted

Which of the following is not a desirable characteristic of an ideal refrigerant?

Correct answer: A
Solution

(a)
The boiling temperature of the refrigerant at atmospheric pressure should be low. If the boiling
temperature of the refrigerant is high at atmospheric pressure, the compressor should be operated
at high vacuum. The high boiling temperature reduces the capacity and operating cost of the
system.
