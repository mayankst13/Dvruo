Not attempted

In vapour absorption refrigeration system, the effects of using aqua-ammonia mixture instead of pure ammonia entering the evaporator is /are:

Correct answer: N/A
Solution

(c, d)
In brief, the effects of aqua-ammonia mixture instead of pure ammonia entering the evaporator are:
(i) Evaporator temperature does not remain constant.
(ii) lower evaporator pressure is required to obtain the given evaporator temperature.
(iii) Refrigeration effect is reduced.
(iv) Some water is left behind in the evaporator, which requires a larger velocity for removal to the absorber.
(v) COP decreasess.
