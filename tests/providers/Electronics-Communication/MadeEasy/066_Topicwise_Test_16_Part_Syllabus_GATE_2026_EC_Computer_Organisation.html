IEEE standard 754 defined single floating point precision formate uses the excess-bias, then the bias is

Correct answer: D
Solution

(d)
In IEEE 754 single-precision floating-point format, the bias is calculated as
Bias = 2(k – 1) – 1
where k is the number of bits in exponent. Thus, bias is one less than the center of the exponent range.
